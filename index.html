Welcome to The Motivated Connection

Our Goal is to unite like- minded business professionals who are intrinsically Motivated to grow their businesses. The Motivated Connection exists to promote an environment that is conducive to helping members increase productivity, revenue and profitability. We will accomplish this by building authentic relationships, promoting education, growing our sphere of influence, serving our communities and supporting our fellow MC Peers.
